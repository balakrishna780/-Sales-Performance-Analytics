💹 Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

The Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Microsoft Power BI solution designed to monitor and analyze sales performance across multiple business dimensions. It provides executives and business stakeholders with actionable insights into revenue trends, product performance, regional sales distribution, and category-wise contributions, enabling data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Project Objectives
Monitor overall sales performance through key business KPIs.
Analyze sales trends over multiple years.
Identify top-performing and underperforming products.
Evaluate sales contribution across categories and sub-categories.
Compare regional sales performance.
Provide interactive filtering for dynamic business analysis.
